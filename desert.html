Meerkat

image from https://www.bbcearth.com/

A meerkat is a mammal and an omnivore. They have bushy brown striped fur. Its face is small and pointed with large eyes surrounded by dark patches. Long sharp claws help build their burrows, where they live. Interesting fact, they can recognize each other's voice. They start their day by grooming or laying in the sun. The rest of their day they just find food. One meerkat will watch the others meerkats back by signaling them when a predator comes.

(source from https://kids.nationalgeographic.com/animals/mammals/facts/meerkat & https://www.livescience.com/)

Rattlesnake

image from https://kids.nationalgeographic.com/

A rattlesnake is a reptile and a carnivore. It has a scaly body and splotches of brown-ish, yellow-ish, black-ish, and grey-is colors. At the tip of the snakes tail is its rattle. Intersting fact, their rattle adds another rattle segment when finished shedding. All day they just wait for a small animals. They are active during the daytime, though when its really hot they are actived during the night.
