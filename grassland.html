Giraffe

image from https://chantecaille.com/blogs/le-magazine/help-us-stand-tall-for-giraffes

A giraffe is a mammal and a herbivore. They are known for there very long necks. Also there long legs. Their coat is tan, white, or yellow with brown spot shapes. At the end of their tail there is bunch of hair. Interesting fact, giraffe's only drink water once a few days because of their long necks. Girrafes are ruminants so they eat leaves, barks, and twigs-45kg a day. They spend they day eating and just standing.

Lion

image from https://www.nationalgeographic.com/

A lion is a mammal and a carnivore. Their bodies are strong and their legs are powerful. Their coats are a yellow gold-ish color. It is said that male lions have more hair than female lions. The hair only covers the head, neck, and underneath the stomach area. Interesting fact, female lions do most of the hunting. Active during night, during the day they relax, rest, and sleep. They also eat a big meal by stalking their prey.
