Jellyfish

image from https://www.discovermagazine.com/

A jellyfish is an invertebrate and a they eat many different things. Jellyfishes are made up 95% of water so they are clear though some are pink, yellow, blue, and yellow. They have no brain, heart, bones, and eyes. Their just like a bag with arms. Interesting fact, not all jellyfish has tentacles. When its daytime they go deep in the water for safety. When it night they head back up to eat on planktons.

Seahorse

image from https://www.wildrepublic.com/

A seahorse is a fish and a carnivore. Their head is horse shaped and its tail is flexible, it can hold on to things. Their eyes don't move together it each moves on its own. Interesting fact, these little seahorses has a big appetite. They swim in pairs and change colors of their body to stay away from predators. Seahorses also do a dance for each other as a signal or something.
